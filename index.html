📋 Before You Go to Malang

  • Valid passport / national ID
  • Indonesian Rupiah (IDR) cash — ATMs available but bring some
  • Travel insurance covering medical emergencies
  • Accommodation bookings confirmed
  • Download Google Maps offline for Malang area
  • Download Gojek / Grab app for local transport
  • Local SIM card or international roaming plan
  • Sunscreen SPF 50+ (tropical sun is intense)
  • Light rain jacket (afternoon showers common)
  • Mosquito repellent
  • Basic medication: paracetamol, antidiarrheal, antihistamine
  • Reusable water bottle (stay hydrated)
  • Power adapter (Indonesia uses Type C/F, 220V)
  • Emergency contact numbers saved
  • Modest clothing for visiting temples/mosques

🌞 Dry Season (May–Oct)

Best time to visit. Clear skies, lower humidity. Temperatures 18–28°C. Perfect for Bromo and outdoor activities.

Best Season

🌧️ Wet Season (Nov–Apr)

Afternoon showers daily. Morning usually clear. Lush green landscapes. Lower tourist crowds and hotel rates.

Budget Season

🏔️ Mountain Areas (Bromo)

Temperatures drop to 5–10°C at night near Bromo crater. Bring warm layers even in dry season.

Pack Warm

👕 What to Wear in Malang

☀️ City Exploration

Light breathable clothing — cotton t-shirts, shorts or light trousers. Comfortable walking shoes. Sunglasses and hat essential.

Casual

🕌 Temples & Mosques

Cover shoulders and knees. Long trousers or skirt. Many sites provide sarongs for loan. Remove shoes before entering.

Respectful

🏔️ Mount Bromo / Semeru

Warm jacket or fleece essential — temperatures drop below 10°C at dawn. Gloves recommended. Sturdy hiking boots.

Warm Layers

🌧️ Rainy Days

Compact foldable rain jacket or poncho. Quick-dry clothing. Waterproof sandals useful. Most rain passes within 30–60 minutes.

Rain Ready

🍽️ Restaurants & Malls

Smart casual acceptable everywhere. Air conditioning in malls can be very cold — bring a light cardigan.

Smart Casual

📸 Instagram Spots

Malang's Kampung Warna-Warni and flower gardens are very popular. Bright colors photograph beautifully!

Colorful

🧳 Essential Equipment to Carry

📱 Smartphone

Google Maps, Gojek, Grab, Google Translate offline. Keep fully charged. Bring powerbank.

Essential

🔋 Powerbank

Minimum 10,000mAh. Charging points not always available at tourist sites. Full day drains battery fast.

Essential

💵 Cash (IDR)

Many local warungs and markets don't accept cards. Carry Rp200,000–500,000 daily. BNI, BCA, Mandiri ATMs available.

Essential

🌂 Compact Umbrella

Afternoon rain showers can appear suddenly. A foldable umbrella fits any bag.

Recommended

🧴 Sunscreen & Insect Repellent

SPF 50+ for outdoor activities. DEET-based repellent for evening and jungle areas.

Health

💊 Basic Medication

Paracetamol, oral rehydration salts, antidiarrheal, antihistamine, antiseptic wipes. Pharmacies widespread.

Health

🗺️ Top Destinations in & Around Malang

🌋 Mount Bromo

Indonesia's most iconic volcanic landscape. Sunrise from Penanjakan point is unforgettable. Book jeep tours the night before. 2 hours from Malang.

Must Visit

🎨 Kampung Warna-Warni

Jodipan & Ksatrian colorful villages along the Brantas River. Free to enter. Most photogenic spot in Malang city.

Instagram Spot

🏛️ Singosari Temple

13th century Hindu temple from the Singosari Kingdom. Free entry. Respectful dress required.

History

🌸 Coban Rondo Waterfall

Beautiful waterfall surrounded by pine forest, 30km northwest of Malang. Easy hiking trail. Entry ~Rp15,000.

Nature

🏖️ Balekambang Beach

Known as "Bali of Malang" — small Hindu temple on rocky island offshore. 65km south. Dramatic scenery.

Beach

🍎 Batu Apple Orchards

Malang region famous for apples and strawberries. Pick your own fruit at Agro Wisata farms in Batu.

Experience

🏰 Alun-Alun Malang

Central city square with Dutch colonial architecture, Grand Mosque, government buildings. Evening food vendors. Free.

City Center

🦁 Jatim Park 1, 2 & 3

Three world-class theme parks in Batu — science museum, wildlife park, and water park. Book tickets online for discounts.

Family

🌺 Selecta Recreation Park

Colonial-era flower garden and recreation park in Batu city. Beautiful rose gardens. Entry ~Rp35,000.

Family

🚗 Getting Around Malang

🛵 Gojek / Grab

Most convenient option. Book via app — motorbike (GoRide) or car (GoCar). Clear pricing, no haggling. Download before arriving.

Rp10,000–60,000 typical ride

🚐 Angkot (Minibus)

Local shared minibuses connecting all areas. Very cheap but routes can be confusing. Ask locals for help.

Rp4,000–7,000 per journey

🚕 Blue Bird Taxi

Metered taxis — reliable and safe. Blue Bird is the most trusted brand in Indonesia. Good for airport transfers.

Meter starts Rp7,500

🚗 Car Rental + Driver

Best option for Bromo and day trips outside city. Arrange through hotel or trusted local travel agents.

Rp400,000–700,000/day

🚂 Train

Malang connects to Surabaya (2h), Yogyakarta (7h), Jakarta (15h). Book via KAI Access app. Comfortable AC trains.

Rp80,000–450,000

✈️ Airport (ABD Saleh)

Domestic flights to Jakarta, Bali, Makassar. 18km from city center. Taxis and Grab available outside arrivals.

Taxi: Rp80,000–120,000

📋 Do's & Don'ts in Malang

✅ Do's

  • Greet locals with "Selamat pagi/siang/sore"
  • Remove shoes before entering homes and mosques
  • Use right hand to give/receive money and food
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites
  • Bargain politely at traditional markets
  • Try local street food — bakso, rawon, rujak cingur
  • Download Gojek and Grab apps before arriving
  • Carry small denomination IDR cash
  • Book Bromo jeep tours the evening before
  • Respect Friday prayer times (12–1pm) near mosques
  • Ask permission before photographing people
  • Stay hydrated — tropical heat is deceptive

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't use left hand for eating or giving items
  • Don't touch someone's head — considered sacred
  • Don't point with index finger — use thumb instead
  • Don't raise your voice or show anger publicly
  • Don't wear revealing clothing at temples
  • Don't drink tap water — bottled water only
  • Don't accept unofficial taxis without fixed price
  • Don't ignore motorcycle traffic — roads can be chaotic
  • Don't litter — significant fines apply
  • Don't photograph military/police facilities
  • Don't disrespect the call to prayer (Adzan)
  • Don't ignore entrance fees at temples

🛒 Traditional Markets & Shopping

🏪 Pasar Besar Malang

Main traditional market. Fresh produce, spices, local snacks, batik fabrics. Opens 5am–5pm. Lively and authentic.

City Center

🌺 Pasar Bunga (Flower Market)

One of East Java's largest flower markets. Best visited early morning for freshest stock. Jl. Kyai Tamin area.

Unique

🍎 Pasar Batu (Batu City)

Famous for fresh apples, strawberries and vegetables from the highlands. Best prices for Malang's famous produce.

Fresh Produce

🎨 Pasar Seni Gajahmada

Art and souvenir market. Local handicrafts, batik clothing, Malang traditional masks (Topeng Malang). Great for gifts.

Souvenirs

🦐 Pasar Ikan (Fish Market)

Fresh seafood market. Restaurants nearby will cook your purchases. Best visited in the morning.

Seafood

🏬 Malang Town Square (MATOS)

Modern shopping mall. Branded goods, cinema, food court. Air conditioned comfort. Grab/Gojek friendly.

Modern Mall
